01/ DocketChat

OVERVIEW

This case study explores the challenges users face when seeking legal assistance online and how an AI-powered chatbot can provide an accessible, user-friendly solution. Many individuals struggle with understanding complex legal terminology, navigating legal resources, and trusting AI-generated advice. As a result, they often become overwhelmed and abandon the platform before receiving the help they need.

Role

UI Designer

Time

3 Weeks

THE PROCESS

This case study covers the problem analysis, user research, ideation, UI/UX design process, and final solutions, showcasing how thoughtful design can enhance accessibility and usability in legal tech.

PROBLEM

Many platforms overwhelm users with excessive customization options or fail to communicate the limitations of AI-generated legal advice. This leads to frustration, confusion, and lack of trust, causing users to abandon the website before receiving the legal help they need.

SOLUTION

Create a simple and easy-to-use chat platform that clearly explains what the AI can and cannot do. Use plain language, helpful prompts, and options to connect with a real person if needed. This helps users feel more confident, less confused.

EMPHATHIZE

RESEARCH GOALS

Before diving into design, I outlined clear research goals to help guide my discovery process. These goals focused on understanding the legal assistance landscape, user needs, and technology expectations.

  • Understand the current trends in online legal assistance and AI-driven services
  • Identify the target users for AI legal chat tools
  • Analyze competitors in the legal tech space, including strengths and weaknesses
  • Explore how users currently search for legal help online
  • Understand the emotional and practical challenges users face during legal issues
  • Discover pain points in the online experience of accessing affordable legal guidance

MARKET RESEARCH

To support these goals, I conducted secondary research and analyzed user behavior patterns in the legal assistance space, particularly in urgent or sensitive situations like housing disputes.

  • AI legal tools are gaining traction, especially for handling simple or repetitive legal questions.
  • Many users start with Google to find legal help, then end up overwhelmed by articles or legal jargon.
  • There is a growing expectation for chatbot interfaces to provide quick, human-like responses.
  • Trust and privacy are key concerns when sharing legal information with online tools.
  • People value clear, action-oriented answers and the option to connect with a professional if needed.

USER PERSONA

Based on my research into the needs of everyday people facing unexpected legal issues, I developed a user persona to guide my design decisions. Meet Ece — a law student in her early 20s who balances her studies with part-time work. Although she’s gaining legal knowledge, she still finds it challenging to interpret real-world legal documents and processes, especially under pressure. When she faces issues like confusing landlord notices or contract questions, she looks for fast, reliable support. A platform like this not only empowers her with accessible legal guidance, but also boosts her confidence when navigating tricky situations. Ece’s experience reflects a growing group of young adults who are informed but still need help bridging the gap between legal knowledge and everyday application.

DEFINE & IDEATE

After understanding the user’s needs and challenges, I defined the main problem: many people struggle to find clear and trustworthy legal help online. Based on this, I started brainstorming simple, helpful solutions that would make the experience less confusing and more supportive. This included features like easy-to-understand answers, clear navigation, and ways to connect with real legal support if needed.

FINAL REVIEW

In this project, I designed DocketChat, an AI-powered legal research platform aimed at helping users — including lawyers, judges, and legal researchers — quickly access court documents, dockets, case law, and legal opinions. Through user research, I discovered that many users feel overwhelmed by complex legal terminology and have difficulty finding relevant information quickly. Based on these findings, I focused on creating a simple, intuitive interface, reducing legal jargon, offering clear navigation, and including features like AI-enhanced search, real-time updates, and the ability to escalate to human legal experts if needed. The final product balances powerful AI tools with user-friendly design, making legal research faster, easier, and more accessible for everyone.
