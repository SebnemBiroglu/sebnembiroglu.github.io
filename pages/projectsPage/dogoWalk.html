02/ DogoWalk

OVERVIEW

DogoWalk is a pet care app designed to provide dog owners with a reliable and secure platform to find professionally trained dog walkers. The app ensures trust and safety by offering background-checked, certified walkers and real-time tracking features. Additionally, DogoWalk provides its own training sessions for dog walkers, ensuring they meet high standards of care and handling. This case study explores the user experience design process behind DogoWalk, addressing pain points and designing solutions for both dog owners and walkers.

THE PROCESS

The design process focused on creating an intuitive, reliable, and emotionally connected experience that fosters trust and enhances the relationship between pet owners and walkers.

PROBLEM

Pet owners often face the challenge of finding trustworthy, reliable walkers who understand their pet's needs. This problem is heightened by concerns around transparency, safety, and communication during the walk. There’s a lack of clear ways to evaluate the skills and care philosophy of walkers, which often leaves pet owners feeling unsure about who to trust with their beloved pets.

SOLUTION

Builds trust by offering verified walker profiles, real-time tracking, and live updates, ensuring pet owners feel connected during walks. The standout feature is training for walkers, where each walker undergoes specialized training, including emergency care and pet behavior, to ensure the highest quality of care.

EMPHATHIZE

RESEARCH GOALS

Before diving into research, I defined a set of clear objectives and created a plan to guide the process.

  • Understand the current landscape of the pet care and dog walking industry
  • Identify TrustPaws’ target user groups: pet owners and dog walkers
  • Explore how trust is currently built (or lost) on existing platforms
  • Evaluate the strengths and weaknesses of competitors
  • Uncover how users choose walkers and what builds long-term confidence
  • Discover pain points in both the online booking experience and the in-person walking process

MARKET RESEARCH

The pet care industry is booming, especially in urban areas. With increasing pet ownership and busy lifestyles, more people are turning to dog walking services. However, studies and online sentiment reveal a significant gap in trust and transparency within this sector.

Key insights from market data and trend reports:

  • Over 60% of pet owners express hesitation when using on-demand pet care apps
  • Trust and safety are consistently rated as top concerns when outsourcing pet care
  • Apps like Rover and Wag dominate the market, but are often seen as transactional and impersonal
  • This suggests a growing opportunity for a platform that focuses not just on convenience—but relationship building and care quality.

USER INTERVIEWS

To gain deeper insights into user pain points, we conducted qualitative interviews with:

  • Dog owners: Expressed concerns about safety, lack of walker qualifications, and insufficient real-time updates.
  • Aspiring and professional dog walkers: Highlighted the need for better training, recognition, and a transparent booking process.
  • “I feel anxious leaving my pet with a stranger.”
  • “I want to show I’m serious, but there’s no space for that.”
  • “I wish I had more control or insight.”
  • “Training would help me feel more confident.”
  • “Reviews don’t tell me how prepared someone is.”
  • “Apps treat me like a number, not a professional.”

USABILITY TESTING

Goals

After building prototypes of DogoWalk, I conducted usability testing to ensure that the app’s experience aligned with the needs and expectations of users like Selin. My primary goals were to:

  • Test how easily users can book a walk with a trained walker
  • Understand how users engage with live walk updates and summaries
  • Evaluate trust signals in walker profiles and training credentials
  • Identify any friction in creating and editing a pet profile
  • Observe emotional reactions to communication and transparency features

Method

Participants - 3 dog owners, ages 30-40 who regularly use pet services Format - Remote moderated sessions via Zoom (20-30 minutes each) Tasks Tested:

  • Create a pet profile
  • Browse and book a walker
  • View live walk tracking
  • Read a post-walk summary
  • Message a walker
  • Tools: Figma prototype

FINAL REVIEW

This project focused on creating a user-friendly and trustworthy experience for both pet owners and walkers. The UX/UI design was guided by user research and feedback, resulting in clear navigation, clean visuals, and features that directly address user needs—such as certified walker training, detailed profiles, and easy booking. The interface was designed to feel safe, professional, and approachable, helping users feel confident in their choices. Overall, the app provides a reliable and intuitive platform that enhances trust, simplifies pet care services, and sets a new standard in the pet app industry.
